Eating Well and Healthy

The sacrosanct “eat 5 fruits and vegetables a day” is a rule that I’m sure you’ve heard dozens and dozens of times, but there’s a reason for that. These fruits and vegetables have a protective role in the prevention of diseases that can appear at age, such as cancers, cardiovascular diseases, obesity, diabetes… They also provide the vitamins, minerals and fibers necessary for the proper functioning of the body.m.

Adice -Eating

Exercise regularly

Again, I’m sure you’ve heard that a lot. But regular physical activity is essential for your health, and for all ages. Physical activity helps maintain the body and mind, reduces mortality and also prevents the onset of certain diseases such as cancer, cardiovascular disease, diabetes, or being overweight. It also helps to fight stress and sleep better.

Advice -Exercising

Drink a lot of water

Water is essential for our survival. As a reminder, our body is composed of 60 to 70% water. Drinking water thus helps to maintain a certain physical form (even if it is not enough to be healthy, of course), ensures us a good mental form also and has health virtues that you surely do not suspect. So you have to make sure you have enough water in a day to be on top. Doctors recommend drinking between 1.5 and 2 litre(s) of water per day for an adult. A figure that can be revised upwards in the event of hot weather like this summer or if you practice a sport, in order to hydrate yourself.

Advice -The importance of water

Get plenty of sleep

Do you know that you spend almost 25 years of your life sleeping? That is to say if sleep has a preponderant part in our existence and should not be neglected. Lack of sleep can affect your mood, your mental state, your physical state, can disrupt your metabolism or even increase the risk of developing certain diseases.
